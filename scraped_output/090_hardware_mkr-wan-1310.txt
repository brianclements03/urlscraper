MKR WAN 1310

The Arduino MKR WAN 1310 provides a practical and cost effective solution to add LoRa® connectivity to projects requiring low power. This open source board can connect to the Arduino Cloud, your own LoRa-based network using the Arduino LoRa® PRO Gateway, existing public networks such as The Things Network or even other boards using the direct connectivity mode.

The MKR WAN 1310 brings LoRa® connectivity to your projects. Using the popular Arm® Cortex®-M0 32-bit SAMD21 processor, it also features the powerful Murata CMWX1ZZABZ module and the ECC508 crypto-chip for security. The board is part of the MKR family, where you can choose from a large variety of shields to build projects out of the box with minimal effort!
